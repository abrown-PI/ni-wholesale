Recommended Stacking Protocols

These products are designed to work together. Here are common recommendations:

Total Gut Restoration (Core Stack): Ultra Spore Biotic + Ultra Pre Powder — start the probiotic first (2 weeks), then add the prebiotic.

Digestive Comfort: Add GastroGuard for patients with bloating, reflux, or upper GI discomfort. Ideal for PPI taper patients.

Antibiotic Recovery: FloraRestore during and after antibiotic therapy. Follow up with the core stack for full restoration.

Women's Health: FemFlora Balance + Ultra Spore Biotic for recurrent vaginal or UTI concerns, especially alongside BHRT.

GLP-1 Support Protocol: Ultra Spore Biotic + Ultra Pre Powder + GastroGuard — comprehensive gut support for patients on semaglutide or tirzepatide experiencing GI side effects.
