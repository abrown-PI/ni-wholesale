Privacy Policy

1. What we collect

We collect information you provide when applying for a wholesale account and placing orders:

  • Business identity: company name, pharmacy type, state license number, DEA registration, NPI, tax ID / EIN, resale certificate

Payment card details are handled by our payment processor (currently unassigned; historically Stripe) and are not stored on our servers.

2. How we use it

  • Verify your eligibility for wholesale purchasing (licensing checks)

We do not sell your information.

We retain account and transaction records as required by federal and state pharmacy, tax, and business regulations — typically 7 years. You can request deletion of non-required data by emailing us; regulatory-required records will remain.

Cookie Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-07

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by the websites you visit. They let sites remember what you've done (like signing in or filling a cart) between page loads and between sessions.

2. Cookies we use

Strictly necessary: we use browser localStorage and session cookies to keep you signed in, remember your cart contents, remember which billing entity you're purchasing for, and preserve site settings. These are essential to the wholesale ordering flow — if you disable them, the site will not function.

Analytics and marketing: we do not currently use third-party analytics or marketing/advertising cookies. If that changes, this page will be updated and we will re-prompt for consent where required.

3. Managing cookies

You can clear cookies and localStorage at any time from your browser's privacy settings. Doing so will sign you out and empty your cart on this site.

Product Quality

Manufacturing partners, quality assurance, and Certificates of Analysis

Manufacturing partners

Nutritional Innovations is a distributor — we do not manufacture supplements ourselves. Instead we partner with FDA-registered contract manufacturers in the United States that operate under current Good Manufacturing Practices (cGMP) as codified in 21 CFR Part 111.

Our private-label microbiome line (Ultra Spore Biotic, Ultra Pre Powder, GastroGuard, FloraRestore, FemFlora Balance) is manufactured in partnership with Microbiome Labs. Our broader vitamin and mineral catalog is produced under strict cGMP controls by FDA-registered facilities in the United States.

Quality standards our partners follow

  • 21 CFR Part 111 cGMP compliance for dietary supplements
  • Identity, purity, strength, and composition testing on every lot
  • Documented raw material specifications and vendor qualification
  • Environmental controls and staff training under a documented quality system

Certificates of Analysis (CoA)

A Certificate of Analysis documents the results of testing for a specific manufactured lot. We can provide a CoA for any product lot shipped to your pharmacy.

To request a CoA, email quality@nutritionalinnovations.net with the product name and lot number (printed on the bottle or shipping documentation). We aim to respond within 3 business days.

Reporting a quality concern

If you receive a product that appears damaged, mislabeled, or otherwise fails to meet expectations, contact quality@nutritionalinnovations.net immediately. Adverse events should also be reported to FDA MedWatch at fda.gov/safety/medwatch.
