Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-03

1. What we collect

We collect information you provide when applying for a wholesale account and placing orders:

  • Business identity: company name, pharmacy type, state license number, DEA registration, NPI, tax ID / EIN, resale certificate
  • Contact information: names, emails, phone numbers, billing and shipping addresses
  • Transaction data: orders placed, invoices, payments
  • Site usage: pages visited, browser type, referring URL, IP address

Payment card details are handled by our payment processor (currently unassigned; historically Stripe) and are not stored on our servers.

2. How we use it

  • Verify your eligibility for wholesale purchasing (licensing checks)
  • Process and fulfill orders, invoices, and returns
  • Communicate with you about your account, orders, and products
  • Comply with DSCSA, DEA, state pharmacy board, and tax reporting requirements
  • Detect and prevent fraud

3. Who we share it with

We share information only with parties needed to run the business:

  • Payment processors (for card payments)
  • Shipping carriers (name, address, phone for delivery)
  • Accounting and ERP systems (NetSuite) for invoicing
  • Regulators upon lawful request

We do not sell your information.

4. Data retention

We retain account and transaction records as required by federal and state pharmacy, tax, and business regulations — typically 7 years. You can request deletion of non-required data by emailing us; regulatory-required records will remain.

5. Security

We use industry-standard controls including HTTPS/TLS in transit, encrypted database storage, and least-privilege access. No system is perfectly secure — you agree to notify us immediately at orders@nutritionalinnovations.net if you suspect account compromise.

6. Your choices

You may update your business or contact info at any time from your account. You may opt out of non-transactional email at any time; transactional emails (order confirmations, shipment notices, invoicing) are necessary for the service.

7. Changes

We may revise this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted at the top of this page with the updated effective date.
